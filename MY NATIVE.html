About Chennai

Chennai is the capital city of Tamil Nadu and one of India's most vibrant metropolitan cities. Known as the "Gateway to South India", Chennai is famous for its beautiful Marina Beach, magnificent temples, rich cultural heritage, delicious South Indian cuisine, and rapidly growing IT industry. The city perfectly blends tradition with modern development, making it one of the best places to live, work, and visit. Chennai is also recognized for its educational institutions, healthcare facilities, classical music, Bharatanatyam dance, and warm hospitality.
